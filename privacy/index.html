Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Flingua is a vocabulary flashcard game. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and who we share it with. We have a privacy-first approach — we collect only what is needed to make the game work.

1. Who We Are

Flingua is an independently developed mobile app. For privacy questions, contact us at supportflingua@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following data for signed-in users:

DataWhy
Email addressAccount creation and sign-in
Display nameYour chosen in-app name
Avatar selectionProfile customisation
Language preferencesPre-filling new deck settings
Streak and session countsDaily streak tracking and goal progress
Per-word correct guess countsSmart card prioritisation — showing you words you need more practice on
Membership tier (free / member / supporter)Unlocking paid features
Cosmetic unlock progressTracking which items you have unlocked via ad watches

Guest users (playing without an account) have language preferences and deck data stored locally on their device only. Nothing is sent to our servers until they create an account.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No usage analytics or behavioural tracking
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA / GAID) — all ads are served in non-personalised mode
  • No precise location data
  • No device fingerprinting
  • No data is sold to third parties

4. Third-Party Services

Flingua uses the following third-party services, each of which may receive some of your data as part of how they operate:

ServicePurposeData they receive
Supabase Database and authentication Account details, game progress, and all data listed in section 2. Data is stored on Supabase-managed servers (AWS). See Supabase Privacy Policy.
Google AdMob Serving ads to free users Device model, OS version, IP address (used to derive approximate country), and app version. We request non-personalised ads only — AdMob does not use your advertising ID or build a profile of you for targeted advertising. See Google Privacy Policy. Ads are not shown to Member or Supporter users.
RevenueCat In-app purchase management Your anonymous user ID and purchase history (which product you purchased, when). This is required to verify and restore purchases. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

We do not use Apple Sign In or Google Sign In to access your contacts, calendar, photos, or any data beyond what is needed to authenticate you.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us — we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Guest data (stored locally on your device) is deleted when you uninstall the app.

6. Children

Flingua is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights including access to your data, correction, deletion, and portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Flingua after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests: supportflingua@gmail.com
