Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases after reinstalling? Open the app, go to your Profile, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used when you originally purchased.
I bought Member / Supporter but the app still shows Free tier. Pull-to-refresh on the Profile screen or sign out and back in — this triggers a tier refresh. If the issue persists, tap Restore Purchases on the Profile screen. Still stuck? Contact us below.
How does fling right / left / up / down work?
  • Fling right — you know the word ✓
  • Fling left — you don't know it
  • Fling down — skip for now (comes back later in the session)
  • Fling up or double-tap — flip card to reveal the translation
My streak broke but I played today. Streaks are counted per calendar day. If you played just after midnight on a new day, yesterday's streak may not have been recorded. Unfortunately streaks can't be manually restored. Keep going — you've got this!
How do I delete my account and data? Email us at the address below with your request. We'll delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.
Can I play offline? Offline play is available to Member and Supporter users. Words are cached automatically when you start a game on a connection — if you lose connectivity mid-session, cached words keep you going.
How do I replay the tutorial? Go to Settings (tap the gear icon on the main menu) and tap Replay Tutorial.
